    Me

    Hi, I'm a Web Developer from The Netherlands. I moved to Logan Utah because I love the outdoors. So when I'm not behind my computer you can usually find me in the mountains. I love hiking, climbing, backpacking, mountain biking, skiing and snowboarding.

    I’ve started programming a few years ago. The first few months I taught myself with FreeCodeCamp in evenings and weekends next to my job but as I learned more and got excited about programming, I knew I wanted to change careers and started to code full time. I told my employer (cad2reality) where I was working as a Graphic Designer/3D modeler that I was learning to program and he put me in charge of creating a new website for the company. And that was the start of my career in development!

    I’ve taken many online courses and done lots of projects since than, I have made a selection you can view in the Projects section. I enjoy helping others online as they start learning web development.

    I have a deep understanding of HTML, CSS and JavaScript and have worked with different frameworks like React and KnockoutJS.
